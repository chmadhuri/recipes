Ingredients:
 - 200 ml condensed milk
 - 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
 - 1  teaspoons baking powder
 - 1  teaspoons baking soda
 - 1 cup milk
 - 1 teaspoons vanilla extract
 - 4 tablespoon butter

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour one 9-inch round baking
    pans.
 
 2. In a large bowl, combine the sugar, flour, butter, baking powder, baking soda.
 	Add condensed milk, milk, and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
    Pour batter into prepared pan.
 
 3. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out
    clean. Cool completely.
